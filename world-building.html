World-Building Page

1. Where does this take place?

Example: "This takes place in the floating city of Aeria, a metropolis suspended in the sky by ancient, forgotten technology."

2. What are the rules of the world?

Example: "Magic is forbidden, and anyone caught practicing it is immediately exiled to the Wastelands."

3. What happens if someone breaks these rules?

Example: "If someone is caught using magic, their memories are wiped, and they are forced to work in the city's underbelly, never allowed to see the sky again."
