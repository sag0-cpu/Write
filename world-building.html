Example 1: Describe a Unique Ecosystem

The Enchanted Forest is home to bioluminescent plants that glow softly at night, providing natural light for the creatures that inhabit it. The forest's canopy is so dense that sunlight rarely penetrates, creating a twilight atmosphere during the day.

Example 2: Create a Culture or Society

The Sky Nomads are a civilization that lives aboard massive, floating airships. Their society revolves around trade and exploration, and they have unique customs, such as storytelling through aerial acrobatics.
